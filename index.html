About

My beautiful full-size cello is for sale. It was made in 1969, and the inside placard reads “R. Masens, Lettonia,” which places its origin in Latvia. I haven’t been able to find a lot of information about the builder, as there isn’t a lot written in English.

I’ve used it on many concerts and recording sessions, and I’ve finally outgrown it after 13 years of playing it. I have a new cello now, and I would like to find a fitting home for this instrument.

The pictures I took have a French bridge installed, but since these studio photos were taken, I’ve had a Belgian bridge cut for it. The sound is much fuller now. As well, I had all the varnish retouched. It will come with the original French bridge I had.
